Apple Head's Full Mission

Apple Head, a skilled detective dog, is tasked with tracking down the infamous Coconut and Brutus. She knows each of their strengths and weaknesses, making her the perfect dog for the job.

With Coconut’s intimidating bark and Brutus’s aggressive stance, Apple Head faces a tough challenge. Will she be able to outsmart them? Follow her journey as she navigates tricky battlegrounds, applies her detective skills, and brings Coconut and Brutus to justice!
